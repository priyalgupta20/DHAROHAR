Government Schemes

Prime Minister's Employment Generation Programme

Objective: To generate self-employment and create job opportunities in rural and urban areas by supporting the establishment of micro-enterprises.

Financial Assistance: Provides subsidy support and bank loans, with subsidy rates of 25% for general category and 35% for special categories (SC/ST, women).

Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana - National Rural Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NRLM)

This scheme aims at empowering women in rural areas. Financial Assistance: Provides subsidy support and bank loans, with subsidy rates of 25% for general category and 35% for special categories (SC/ST, women).

Scheme Title 3

This scheme provides financial aid for education. Financial Assistance: Provides subsidy support and bank loans, with subsidy rates of 25% for general category and 35% for special categories (SC/ST, women).
